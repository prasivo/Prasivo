Unit I – Introduction to Human Body

Human body ek well-organized system hai jisme cells, tissues, organs aur systems milkar kaam karte hain. Is unit me hum body levels of organization, basic life processes aur homeostasis samajhte hain.

Theory

Cells body ki basic unit hoti hain. Cells milkar tissues, tissues milkar organs aur organs milkar systems banate hain. Homeostasis ka matlab hota hai body ka internal balance maintain rehna.

Main Points

  • Levels of organization
  • Basic life processes
  • Homeostasis concept

Exam Questions

  • Define homeostasis
  • Explain levels of organization
